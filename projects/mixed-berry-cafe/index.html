Our Story

A Berry Special Place

Mixed Berry Café was born from a passion for quality coffee and the natural sweetness of fresh berries. Founded in 2020, our café has grown from a small corner shop to a beloved community gathering space.

We believe in creating a warm, welcoming environment where friends meet, remote workers find inspiration, and berry enthusiasts discover new flavor combinations.
